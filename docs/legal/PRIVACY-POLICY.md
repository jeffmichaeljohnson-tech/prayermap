# PrayerMap Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 22, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 22, 2025

---

## Introduction

PrayerMap ("we," "our," or "us") is a non-profit ministry platform that enables users to share prayer requests and support one another through a location-based map interface. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

By using PrayerMap, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** — Used for account authentication and optional notifications
- **Display name** (optional) — Shown to other users unless you choose to post anonymously
- **Profile photo** (optional) — Displayed on your profile and with your prayers

If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID email (or a private relay email if you choose "Hide My Email").

### 2. Location Data

PrayerMap is a location-based service. We collect:
- **Precise location** — To place your prayers on the map and show prayers near you
- **Location is required** — The core experience of PrayerMap depends on knowing where prayers are located

You can choose whether to share your exact location or an approximate area when posting prayers. Location data is stored with your prayers to display them on the map for other users.

### 3. User-Generated Content

When you use PrayerMap, we collect the content you create:
- **Prayer requests** — Text, audio recordings, or video recordings you submit
- **Prayer responses** — Your responses to others' prayer requests
- **Messages** — Private conversations with other users
- **Reactions** — Your "Amen" reactions and saved prayers

All content may be posted anonymously if you choose. However, we retain the association internally for moderation purposes.

### 4. Device Information

We automatically collect:
- **Device identifiers** — For push notifications and analytics
- **Device type and OS version** — To ensure app compatibility
- **App version** — For troubleshooting and updates

### 5. Usage Data

We collect information about how you use PrayerMap:
- **App interactions** — Features used, screens viewed, actions taken
- **Crash reports** — Automatically collected to improve stability
- **Performance data** — Load times and responsiveness metrics

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the Service** — Display prayers on the map, enable prayer responses and messaging
2. **Personalize your experience** — Show nearby prayers based on your location
3. **Send notifications** — Alert you to new prayer responses, messages, and nearby prayers (with your permission)
4. **Moderate content** — Screen prayers and messages for inappropriate content to keep the community safe
5. **Improve the Service** — Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues
6. **Communicate with you** — Send important updates about the Service

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Share your location with advertisers
- Monetize your prayer content in any way

---

## Content Moderation

To maintain a safe and respectful community, all user-generated content (prayers, responses, messages) is screened for:
- Inappropriate language
- Hate speech or bullying
- Explicit content
- Spam

Content that violates our guidelines may be removed, and repeat violators may be suspended.

---

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### Publicly Visible Information

When you post a prayer request (not anonymously):
- Your display name and profile photo are visible
- Your prayer content is visible to all users
- The location of your prayer is visible on the map

When you post anonymously:
- Only your prayer content and location are visible
- Your identity is not disclosed to other users

### Service Providers

We share information with third-party services that help us operate PrayerMap:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Database, authentication, storage | Account data, content, usage |
| Mapbox | Map display and location services | Location coordinates |
| Apple | Sign in with Apple authentication | Apple ID (if you use this sign-in method) |
| Amazon Web Services | Media storage and delivery | Audio/video recordings |
| Vercel | Website hosting | Usage analytics |

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect and defend our rights or property
- Prevent wrongdoing in connection with the Service
- Protect the personal safety of users or the public

---

## Data Retention

- **Account data** — Retained while your account is active; deleted upon account deletion request
- **Prayers and responses** — Retained for 1 year after creation, then archived
- **Prayer connection lines** (memorial lines) — Retained for 1 year to visualize the prayer network
- **Messages** — Retained until you delete them or close your account
- **Usage analytics** — Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Portability
You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at privacy@prayermap.net.

### Correction
You can update your profile information directly in the app.

### Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and associated data by:
1. Going to Profile > Settings > Delete Account in the app
2. Emailing us at privacy@prayermap.net

Upon deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

### Notifications
You can manage push notification preferences in your device settings and within the app under Profile > Settings > Notifications.

### Location
Location access is required for core functionality. You can revoke location permission in your device settings, but this will significantly limit app functionality.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Row-level security in our database
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:
- **Right to know** what personal information we collect and how it's used
- **Right to delete** your personal information
- **Right to opt-out** of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- **Right to non-discrimination** for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@prayermap.net.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:
- **Right of access** to your personal data
- **Right to rectification** of inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure** ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to restrict processing**
- **Right to data portability**
- **Right to object** to processing
- **Right to withdraw consent**

Our legal basis for processing your data includes:
- **Consent** — For optional features like notifications
- **Contract** — To provide the Service you've requested
- **Legitimate interests** — For security, fraud prevention, and service improvement

To exercise these rights or lodge a complaint, contact us at privacy@prayermap.net.
