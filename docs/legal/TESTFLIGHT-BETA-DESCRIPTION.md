## Beta App Description (Copy Below)

```
PrayerMap is a free, location-based spiritual platform that makes prayer visible. See where prayer is needed. Send prayer where you are.

WHAT PRAYERMAP DOES
• View an interactive map showing prayer requests in your area
• Post prayer requests (text, audio, or video) for your community
• Respond to others' prayers with encouragement and support
• See beautiful "memorial lines" connecting prayers and responses
• Experience real-time updates as prayer activity happens around you

KEY FEATURES IN THIS BUILD
• Full-screen prayer map with Mapbox integration
• Ethereal theme with glowing prayer connection lines
• TikTok-style vertical swipe Feed for browsing prayers
• Audio and video prayer recording with waveform visualization
• Push notifications for new prayers and responses
• Anonymous posting option for sensitive prayer requests
• Dark mode support

WHAT WE'RE TESTING
• Map performance and prayer marker display
• Audio/video recording quality and playback
• Real-time prayer updates and memorial lines
• Push notification delivery
• Overall app stability and user experience

HOW TO PROVIDE FEEDBACK
• Use the TestFlight feedback feature (shake device or screenshot)
• Report any crashes, bugs, or performance issues
• Share suggestions for improving the prayer experience
• Let us know if any content moderation issues arise

KNOWN LIMITATIONS
• Location access is required for core functionality
• Some features may be incomplete or under development
• Content moderation is active but may not catch everything

PRIVACY & DATA
• Your location is used to display and create prayers on the map
• You can post prayers anonymously
• We do not sell your data or use it for advertising
• Full privacy policy: https://prayermap.net/privacy.html

PrayerMap is a 100% non-profit ministry. We exist solely to connect people through prayer.
```
